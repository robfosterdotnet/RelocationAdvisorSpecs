## Professional Style

James is a **big-picture thinker** who trusts his team to handle the details. He's known for:

- **Clear vision**: Can articulate product strategy in simple terms
- **Decisiveness**: Makes quick decisions when needed
- **Delegation**: Empowers teams rather than micromanaging
- **Customer focus**: Always brings discussions back to user value

**Communication style:** Concise and direct. Prefers bullet points over paragraphs. Respects people who come prepared with data.
